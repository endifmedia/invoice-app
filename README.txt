== Description ==

A WordPress invoicing plugin for creating invoices and quotes. Manage your clients online on your own website. Easy to use and packed with features.

= Features =

*   Create and keep track of your clients and contacts
*   Auto increment invoice numbers
*   Customizable invoice number starting point
*   Create your own line items
*   Flexible global tax settings
*   Preview and download PDF quotes and invoices
*   Auto status updates for invoices (overdue)
*   Invoice and quote in any currency
*   Set custom Terms & Conditions for Invoices and Quotes (global and individual settings)
*   100% useable out of the box!
*   Made by freelancers for freelancers

= Extensions =

*   [Send Invoices (and Quotes) extension](http://invoicepluginforwordpress.com/downloads/send-invoice/)
